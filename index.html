I'm currently a Ph.D. student at Tsinghua University, Department of Computer Science and Technology, advised by Professor Zhengfeng Ji.

I received my Bachelor of Science degree from Peking University, with the summa cum laude title.

My research interest is quantum computing theory, with a focus on quantum networks and quantum error correction, particularly their interplay. I'm also interested in quantum codes and computational complexity, and have devoted time and effort to both.

Visit my blog to learn more.
